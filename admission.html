Admissions

Welcome to the Admissions page of Middlesex University Dubai. We are excited that you are considering joining our vibrant and diverse community. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent to help you every step of the way.

Application Process

  1. Online Application: Start by filling out our online application form.
  2. Submit Documents: Provide all necessary documents including academic transcripts, proof of English proficiency, and a personal statement.
  3. Application Review: Our admissions team will review your application and contact you if any further information is needed.
  4. Interview: You may be invited for an interview as part of the assessment process.
  5. Offer: If your application is successful, you will receive an offer letter with further instructions.

Entry Requirements

To be eligible for admission to Middlesex University Dubai, you must meet the following entry requirements:

  • A high school diploma or equivalent.
  • Proof of English proficiency (e.g., IELTS, TOEFL).
  • Specific course requirements, which can be found on the respective course pages.

Tuition Fees and Scholarships

Our tuition fees vary depending on the program and level of study. We offer a range of scholarships and financial aid options to help make your education affordable. For detailed information on tuition fees and available scholarships, please visit our Tuition Fees page.
