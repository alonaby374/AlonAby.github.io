About Us

Middlesex University Dubai is the first overseas campus of the internationally renowned Middlesex University in London. Since its inception in 2005, the campus has provided a quality UK education to students from all over the world, contributing to the global diversity and cultural richness of our institution.

Our mission is to provide an exceptional educational experience through innovation, research, and engagement. We are committed to nurturing the talents and aspirations of our students, equipping them with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in their careers and make a positive impact in the world.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in everything we do.
  • Integrity: We act with honesty and transparency.
  • Inclusivity: We embrace diversity and provide an inclusive environment for all.
  • Innovation: We encourage creativity and innovation in teaching, learning, and research.
  • Community: We foster a sense of community and social responsibility among our students and staff.

Our Campus

Located in Dubai Knowledge Park, our state-of-the-art campus offers a range of modern facilities including lecture theatres, computer labs, libraries, and recreational areas. Our campus environment is designed to support students' academic and personal growth, ensuring they have access to all the resources they need to thrive.

Global Recognition

Middlesex University Dubai is part of a global network of Middlesex University campuses and partners. Our degrees are recognized worldwide, and our graduates are highly sought after by employers across various industries.
